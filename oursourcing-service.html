Outsourcing Services

Partner for strategic IT managed services to transform operations

Our Outsourcing Support Services program is designed with a vision to make you in charge of your business and we act as an extended arm of your team to ensure that you get maximum return of IT investment with minimal downtime. Our IT Outsourcing start from small help desk management and extend right up to comprehensive IT Infrastructure Management under outsourced mode

Assessing your organizational needs to provide effective outsourcing services

Managed Services

If you have a long term visibility, then our staff augmentation services can be extended to Managed Services Model (Outsourcing). Under this we assume control of all or part of your IT system and sign a Service Level Agreement with committed scope and term and pricing tied service level and volumes. Unlike staff augmentation we will manage your complete deliveries by bringing in our own processes, tools and systems (where required).

Staff Augmentation Services

We can provide highly skilled and certified professional who can work as an extension of your IT team working directly under your command and control. Our skilled professionals can help you to develop, maintain, manage and support your applications and systems. Our staff augmentation services include Database Administrators, Enterprise Application (SAP, Microsoft ERP and Oracle), Major Operating System, Network Administrators and etc.

Co-Management Services Outsourcing

Our Co-Managed Services is a partnership of Services between your internal IT Department and Virtech IT engineers and specialist where you choose what you need. We use this Service Model to fill in the gaps which are missing at your wherein instead of hiring resources you outsource those pieces skills to us.

Application Development Outsourcing

Our Application Development Outsourcing is aimed at Development and Customization of Application or Products for Web, Mobile or Desktop based platforms. We bring in vast experience and global client base across multiple industry domain.
