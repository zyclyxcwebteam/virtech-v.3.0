Cyber Security

Cyber Security

Taking security a step ahead

With a recent spur in cyber-attacks, we have developed an integrated approach to address relevant and predictive incidents. This service is dedicated to the practice of protecting information and data from outside sources on the Internet. We aim to ensure that our clients not only feel safe but also have cybersecurity awareness. The team dedicatedly works towards the protection of networks, servers, intranets, and systems. The common types of cyber-attacks we tackle are Hacking, Malware, Phishing, Spoofing, Ransomware, Spamming.

Pushing the possibilities of the Internet

Log Management and Log Correlation

Our team offers practical solutions to manage massive collections of logs. The centralized system will correlate data from various sources to enable faster data computation. It allows users to Monitor and log data efficiently resulting in optimized system performance.

Endpoint Protection

At VIRTECH our solutions are designed to provide 360-degree protection. Endpoint protection detects, prevents, responds and stops threats at the earliest stages of an attack. We provide a whole suite of security operations to support multiple users and use cases.

Web Protection

We believe that users are everywhere and they rely on quick access to data and cloud applications 24/7. In such scenarios, it’s important to ensure that your data is safe from inbound and outbound threats. Our web protection service takes care of security, complex issues and performance preventing any attacks.

Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT)

Our VAPT suite provides a comprehensive assessment of applications. It provides a detailed report on approaching or ongoing threats enabling the organization to better protect their data and systems from malicious attacks. We simulate an attack and test the network and systems’ resistance as part of Penetration Testing.
