Blockchain

Blockchain

Transforming the world one block at a time

The technology acts as a digital ledger that is built on a P2P system allowing different users to have an unchangeable record of transactions, each of which is time-stamped and connected to the previous block. With the addition of each set of transactions added, the data becomes another block in the chain. It is preferred as it is a write-once technology, making it a verifiable and auditable record of every transaction. We suggest the creation of this for Financial services, Smart Property, Retail sector, etc.
