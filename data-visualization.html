Data Visualization

Bringing business intelligence to life

Data Visualization is a visual representation of data in the form of graphs, charts or other visual forms. It has gained popularity recently as a Business Intelligence tool as it allows the users to observe, browse and understand the trends at a glimpse. This is done to study logical correlations of units, inclinations, tendencies, and patterns, depending on the purpose for which it is done. Beside Data scientists and Analysts, it is also used in Finance, Marketing, Tech, etc to visualize data.
